computer accompaniment
given score for performer and accompaniment, match accompaniment with performance at run time(when the artist is giving the performance), produce accompaniment through music synthesis.

finding note onsets = segment music audio into notes

score alignment

style recognition using machine Learning

human computer music performance: need a bass player

~120dB from threshold of hearing to threshold of pain

objects have modes of vibration. Modes generally decay exponentially. Non-linear coupling between modes can complicate things

Pitch perception is approximately logarithmic
• Musical octave: 2 x frequency
• Divide octave into 12 log-spaced
semitones (half-steps)
• Divide semitones into 100 log- spaced cents
• We are sensitive to about 5 cents(100 cents = half step)

Speed of sound: about 1 ft/ms

Pitch - Frequency (20-20 KHz)
Loudness - Intensity (90 dB)
Timbre - Spectrum (and other)

In signal processing, a comb filter adds a delayed version of a signal to itself, causing constructive and destructive interference. The frequency response of a comb filter consists of a series of regularly spaced notches, giving the appearance of a comb.

bin-size: 每秒可以做多少个window。 如果知道SR和window－size in samples，比如1000Hz 的sample rate，每个window要记100个sample。那一秒就可以记1000/100=10个window，这就是bin－size。如果知道window size in seconds，那就直接1/windowsize。
